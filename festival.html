Festivals of Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is a cultural hub in Andhra Pradesh, celebrating many vibrant festivals throughout the year.

Sankranti

Sankranti is a harvest festival celebrated with kite flying, traditional dances, and family gatherings.

Diwali

The festival of lights, celebrated with lamps, fireworks, and sweets.

Dussehra

Celebrates the victory of good over evil with colorful processions.

Godavari Pushkaralu

Every 12 years, devotees take holy dips in the Godavari River and perform rituals along the ghats of Rajahmundry.

Ugadi

Marks the Telugu New Year and is celebrated with special dishes, prayers, and cultural programs.

Maha Shivaratri

An important Hindu festival celebrated with night-long prayers and devotion at Shiva temples.

Vinayaka Chavithi

Devotees worship Lord Ganesha with clay idols, prayers, and immersion processions during this festival.

Lord Krishna

Celebrates the birth of Lord Krishna with devotional singing, dancing, and reenactments of his childhood.

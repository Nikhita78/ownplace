Festivals of Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is a cultural hub in Andhra Pradesh, celebrating many vibrant festivals throughout the year.

Sankranti

Sankranti is a harvest festival celebrated with kite flying, traditional dances, and family gatherings. It marks the beginning of the harvest season.

Diwali

Diwali, the festival of lights, is celebrated with lamps, fireworks, and sweets. Families decorate homes and share festive meals.

Dussehra

Dussehra celebrates the victory of good over evil. In Rajahmundry, colorful processions, cultural performances, and rituals are held.

Godavari Pushkaralu

Every 12 years, the Godavari Pushkaralu is celebrated with devotees taking holy dips in the river and performing rituals along the ghats of Rajahmundry.

Ugadi

Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year and is celebrated with special dishes like Ugadi Pachadi, prayers, and cultural programs in Rajahmundry.

Maha Shivaratri

Maha Shivaratri is an important Hindu festival celebrated with night-long prayers and devotion at Shiva temples across Rajahmundry.

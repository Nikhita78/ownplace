Welcome to Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is a historic city in Andhra Pradesh, located on the banks of the Godavari River. Known as the Cultural Capital of Andhra Pradesh, it is famous for literature, art, and traditions. The city is also a major center for education, tourism, and river-based culture.

Home Town

Rajahmundry is a vibrant city known for its cultural heritage, scenic river views, literary history, and welcoming people.

City

Situated on the banks of the Godavari River, Rajahmundry is known for the iconic Godavari Bridge, rich traditions, and fast-growing development.

Culture

Rajahmundry has a strong cultural legacy in Telugu literature, music, and drama, earning the title “Cultural Capital of Andhra Pradesh”.

Godavari Bridge

The Godavari Rail-Cum-Road Bridge is one of the most iconic landmarks, connecting Rajahmundry and Kovvur across the river.

Education

Rajahmundry is an educational hub with reputed institutions, universities, and well-established schools and colleges.

Temples

Famous temples include ISKCON Rajahmundry, Markandeya Temple, and many historic shrines reflecting spiritual heritage.

Welcome to Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry, the Cultural Capital of Andhra Pradesh, is a historic city located on the banks of the Godavari River. Famous for literature, art, temples, and festivals, it offers scenic beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Home Town

Rajahmundry is known for its cultural heritage and welcoming people.

City

Situated on the banks of the Godavari River, the city shows rapid urban development.

Culture

Rich legacy in Telugu literature, music, and drama earns its cultural fame.

Godavari Bridge

An iconic landmark connecting Rajahmundry and Kovvur across the river.

Education

The city is home to reputed schools, colleges, and universities.

Temples

ISKCON, Markandeya Temple, and many historic shrines reflect spiritual heritage.

Famous Temples in Rajahmundry

Explore the spiritual heritage of Rajahmundry through its most renowned temples.

ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna and known for its serene spiritual atmosphere.

Markandeya Swamy Temple

An ancient Shiva temple on the banks of the Godavari River.

Visweswara Swamy Temple

Also known as Dakshina Kasi, a historic temple and major pilgrimage site.

Bala Tripura Sundari Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Bala Tripura Sundari, part of the Visweswara temple complex.

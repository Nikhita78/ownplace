Famous Temples in Rajahmundry

ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple in Rajahmundry is dedicated to Lord Krishna and is known for its peaceful spiritual atmosphere and devotional programs.

Markandeya Swamy Temple

An ancient Shiva temple located on the banks of the Godavari River, associated with Sage Markandeya.

Visweswara Swamy Temple

Also known as Dakshina Kasi, this historic temple is an important pilgrimage site in Rajahmundry.

Bala Tripura Sundari Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Bala Tripura Sundari, this temple is part of the Visweswara temple complex.

The Godavari River

The Godavari is the second-longest river in India and is often called the "Dakshina Ganga". Flowing through Rajahmundry, the river adds immense beauty, spirituality, and cultural significance to the city.

Famous Places Along Godavari in Rajahmundry

Dowleswaram Barrage

Engineered by Sir Arthur Cotton, this barrage controls Godavari floods and is a major attraction.

Godavari Ghats

Steps along the river where pilgrims take holy dips during festivals like Pushkaralu.

Kadiyapulanka

A scenic area with lush greenery and parks along the Godavari, popular for family outings.

Godavari Bridge

A famous bridge connecting Rajahmundry, providing scenic river views and photography spots.
